### Use-cases
Interaction is a core gameplay mechanic implemented to transfer data between at least 2 interactable actors, it may differ from project to project but here 
are some examples:
* Interacting with objects in the world such as:
    * opening doors. 
    * Pickup objects(Weapons, ammo).
    * Entering vehicles.
    
* Interacting with other characters:
    * Reviving other players.
    * Executions to kill other players.
